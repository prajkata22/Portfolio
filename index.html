The Professional Narrative

I. The Foundation: A Personal Mission

Growing up, I watched my mother work tirelessly in the healthcare industry. Witnessing the daily challenges patients faced, I identified a critical gap in Medical Emergency Services—especially in a country like India where timely access can be a matter of life and death. I knew throughout my childhood that I had to do something to solve these systemic problems. When I finally had the chance to apply my engineering knowledge, I saw it as the perfect platform to showcase a real solution to the issues I had observed for years. This mission became "Hospital Finder." Winning the Smart India Hackathon was the cherry on top; it was the validation I needed to realize I had to go deeper. That moment pushed me to pursue my Master’s in Computer Science and strive for excellence in this field.

II. The Crucible: Customer Support & Connectivity

At Cloud Software Group, I operated at the frontline of technical support, assisting over 200 customers with complex data virtualization and connectivity issues. I diagnosed network bottlenecks, resolved protocol errors, and ensured seamless data flow. This role honed my ability to troubleshoot under pressure and communicate technical resolutions clearly to dealers and internal representatives.

III. The Evolution: From Support to Strategy

While troubleshooting critical connectivity issues, I noticed a recurring pattern: many "technical" failures were actually rooted in undefined business requirements or misaligned data strategies. I realized I didn't want to just fix systems when they broke; I wanted to ensure they were designed correctly from the start. This drove my transition from Support Engineer to Business Analyst. I moved upstream to define the "what" and "why" before the "how"—using my deep technical background to translate complex business needs into precise, executable data requirements that prevent issues before deployment.

IV. The Philosophy

"Customer Success through Technical Precision." Whether I am supporting a dealer with an equipment upgrade or analyzing connectivity data, my goal is the same: to identify the root cause, implement a robust solution, and ensure the stakeholder has the data they need to succeed.
